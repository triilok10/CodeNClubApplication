Engineering Philosophy

The Foundation of Clean Code: I strictly adhere to Clean Architecture principles, ensuring that business rules are decoupled from frameworks and databases. This separation of concerns not only makes the system testable but also adaptable to changing technology landscapes.

Domain-Driven Design (DDD): Software should model reality. I use DDD to bridge the gap between technical implementation and business complexity, creating a "ubiquitous language" that ensures developers and domain experts are always synchronized.

Scalability Beyond Performance: True scalability isn't just about handling requests per second; it's about checking the codebase's ability to accept new features without regression. I prioritize maintainability and code readability to prevent technical debt from slowing down innovation.
